End User Support








We provide cross-border End User Support Services with central controls in real time based on ITIL V3 with the following performance models:

  • IMAC Service - Install Move, Add and Change services for individual printers, laptops, desktops and hand held peripheral devices.
  • Break and Fix - Through a ticket management system and the escalation and management of incidents.
  • Deskside and Remote Support: Able to provide support to users globally on the phone or deskside.
  • Digitalization: Manage regional and global refresh and digitalization programmes.
  • System Management: Proactive and reactive incident management and resolution.
  • Resources: We provide Level 1, Level 2 and Level 3 Engineers at customer sites globally. Highlight Technology Ltd Engineers are highly skilled, industry certified, security cleared and cover Microsoft and Mac software and hardware..

Release, Deployment & Digitalization

Highlight Technologies Ltd provides a comprehensive release and deployment service of hardware and software. with central controls in real time. The objective of our release and deployment services is to deploy releases into operation and launch effective use of the service in order to deliver value to our clients. We can provide the management and the deployment of an extensive digitalization programme. We offer extensive after care, staff training and support. We endeavor to use local talent and resources wherever possible and develop these resources with bespoke training and services.


Data Centre Maintenance





Highlight Technologies Ltd provides Greenfield and Brownfield Data Centre management and development. We offer a maintenance solution in over 195 countries with a guaranteed operational availability of the referring central server and storage systems. As well as network infrastructure. This service is available for products of all leading manufacturers including: IBM, CISCO, Alcatel, Hewlett Packard, Dell, Fujitsu, Lenovo and Huawei with the following performing modules:

  • Global spare parts management and distribution.
  • On site service support and training.
  • Remote telephone support and troubleshooting.
  • Proactive and reactive incident management and resolution.
